The Plan

A modern farmhouse, built for our family

The Harbor Spring is a 4-bedroom, 2.5-bathroom, 3,031 SF two-story plan from Diyanni Homes. It includes the Gold Package: 3-car garage, 9' main floor ceilings, James Hardie® siding, Pella windows, granite/quartz counters, direct vent fireplace, and an industry-leading 30-year structural warranty — all standard.

We're building Elevation B for the covered porch, premium roof trusses, and farmhouse aesthetics. The plan includes a built-in morning room off the kitchen and a 1st floor study that doubles as our home office.
