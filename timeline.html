Build Phases

What Happens When

Typical Diyanni construction phases over ~10 months

Month 1–2

Site Prep + Foundation

Excavation, footing, basement walls poured, backfill, waterproofing.

Month 3–4

Framing + Rough-ins

Walls go up, roof on, windows installed, plumbing/electrical/HVAC rough-ins.

Month 5

Insulation + Drywall

Insulation installed, drywall hung and finished, primer applied.

Month 6–7

Trim + Cabinets + Tile

Interior trim, cabinets installed, tile in bathrooms, paint, hardwood floors.

Month 8–9

Finishes + Fixtures

Countertops, plumbing fixtures, lighting, appliances, exterior landscaping basics.

Month 10

Punch List + Walkthrough

Final inspection, builder walkthrough, punch list completion, closing.
