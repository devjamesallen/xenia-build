The Decision

Why The Harbor Spring

How we evaluated 10+ floor plans across 6 builders and landed here.

The Process

When we decided to build on our Xenia lot, we cast a wide net — comparing on-your-lot custom builders that serve Greene County, Ohio. The constraints:

  • → Family of 4 (2 adults + 2 young kids)
  • → ~3,000 SF, 2-story, 4 BR
  • → Built on bare land in Xenia Township
  • → $750K all-in budget
  • → Modern farmhouse aesthetic
  • → Frequent guests + work-from-home space
Why Harbor Spring

Five reasons it won

01

All four bedrooms upstairs

Master + two kids' rooms + flex bedroom — all on one floor. The right call for the young-family stage. We want our kids close at night, not across the house from us.

02

1st floor study + morning room built in

Work-from-home space on the main floor. Casual dining off the kitchen with natural light. Both included in the base plan — not upgrades, not afterthoughts.

03

Best-in-class Gold Package

3-car garage, 9' main floor ceilings, 9' basement ceilings, granite countertops, hardwood-style flooring — all standard. Other builders charge upgrades for half of these.

04

Confirmed Xenia pricing

$525,210 base price is locked in for our region. No "contact us for pricing" surprises. We know the number before we sign.

05

Future-proof basement

Full basement with 9' ceilings + egress window + bathroom rough-in installed at build. When the kids are older and we want a real guest suite, finished entertainment space, or home gym, the bones are already there. No retrofitting walls or chasing plumbing.
